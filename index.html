The Problem

The way most teams run field ops is broken.

Scattered WhatsApp messages, lost paper checklists, and zero proof of completion — the old way costs you clients, time, and revenue.

📱

WhatsApp Is Not a Workflow

Job assignments buried in chat threads, no audit trail, no accountability. When a client disputes a job, you have nothing to show.

📋

Paper Checklists Don't Scale

Field agents misplace forms, skip steps, or fill them in after the fact. You have zero visibility until they return to the office — if ever.

⚠️

No Proof When It Matters

Client raises a dispute. Was the job done? Was it done right? Without GPS evidence, photos, and a signed certificate, you can't win that conversation.

Platform Features

Everything you need to run field ops at scale

A tightly integrated suite built around how field service teams actually work — not a collection of bolted-on tools.

📋

Work Order Management

Create, assign, and track work orders end-to-end. Define scope, attach job types, set locations, and link clients — all from a structured admin interface.

✅

Job-Level Tracking

Break work orders into individual jobs with their own status, assignment, and completion state. Know exactly where every job stands, in real time.

🏗️

Vendor & Contractor Management

Maintain a curated contractor network. Track skills, assign jobs, manage commercial terms, and rate performance — all within the platform.

👤

Client Management

Maintain a full client directory with contact details, linked work orders, and job history. Professional service delivery starts with organised client records.

Mobile-First Field Execution

📍

GPS & Photo Evidence Capture

Every job completion is stamped with GPS coordinates and timestamped photos. Dispute resolution becomes instant — you have incontrovertible proof.

✍️

Digital Signature Capture

Capture on-site client or supervisor signatures directly in the mobile wizard. Every completion is formally acknowledged in real time, on-site.

📄

Instant Completion Certificates

Generate branded PDF completion certificates automatically on job approval. Send them to clients without touching a printer or a PDF editor.

🔍

Admin Review & Approval Queue

Review submitted job evidence before certificates are issued. Maintain quality control with a structured approval queue that flags incomplete submissions.

⭐

Vendor Performance Ratings

Rate vendors after each job and build a performance history. Drive accountability across your contractor network with transparent, data-backed ratings.

🏢

Vendor Self-Service Portal

Vendors access their own portal to view assigned jobs, commercials, team members, and profile — reducing admin burden on your team.

📊

Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time overview of work orders, jobs, and vendor activity. Make data-driven decisions with operational metrics always at your fingertips.

👥

Multi-User & Role Management

Invite team members, assign roles, and control access. Every user sees exactly what they need to do their job — nothing more, nothing less.

🏛️

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each client organisation operates in a fully isolated environment with its own data, branding, and configuration. Built from the ground up for multi-client deployments.

AI-Powered Features
